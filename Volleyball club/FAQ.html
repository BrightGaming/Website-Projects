Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the age requirement to join the volleyball club?

We accept members of all ages starting from 8 years old. Our programs are structured based on skill level and age group to ensure the best training experience.

How do I register for a volleyball program?

To register for our programs, simply visit the 'Program' section on our website and choose the program that fits your schedule. You can complete the registration process online.

Are there any tryouts before joining?

For some advanced programs, tryouts are required to assess skill level. However, most of our beginner and intermediate programs are open for registration without tryouts.

What should I bring to my first practice?

Please bring your own water bottle, knee pads, comfortable sportswear, and proper indoor shoes. We provide volleyballs and other equipment during practice sessions.
