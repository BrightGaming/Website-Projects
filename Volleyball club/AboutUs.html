About Us

The Story of Tobe VC

Tobe Volleyball Club was founded in 2022 by Lucifer Chrollo. We started and train in Petaling Jaya at ROF Subang Elite.


In 2023, Chrollo found out his passion was in teaching volleyball, so he decided to commit himself to Tobe Volleyball Club.


Tobe Volleyball Club is still a new volleyball club, but it has a dream to grow and help youth volleyball players play the best volleyball.
